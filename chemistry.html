SOIL EROSION

WHAT IS SOIL EROSION:


Soil erosion is the gradual process of wearing away the top layer of soil

BENEFITS OF SOIL EROSION:

  • Natural amounts of soil erosion can help feed water sources with nutrients.
  • Soil erosion can cleanse soil of useless materials like rotting tree matter.
  • Soil erosion can rejuvenate soils after harvesting crops or removing trees.
  • Eroded soil can be deposited on river floodplains, loess plains, and deltas.
  • Soil erosion can soften riverbanks, which can decrease the odds of flooding.
  • Soil erosion can contribute to the formation of new soil in another area.
